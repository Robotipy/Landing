Development timelines vary based on project complexity, features, and technology stack. A simple project might take 2-4 months, while a complex enterprise system could take a year or more. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial discovery phase.

We primarily use the Agile methodology, which allows us to be flexible and responsive to change. We work in sprints, providing regular updates and demos to ensure the project stays on track and aligned with your vision.

Who owns the source code and intellectual property? expand_more

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance? expand_more

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your application runs smoothly after launch. This can include bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, and feature enhancements.
