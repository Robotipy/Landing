Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming experience for the citizen developer track? expand_more

No, not at all! Our business team training is designed for non-programmers. We focus on low-code/no-code platforms that allow you to build automations using a visual interface.

What specific technologies do you teach in the technical track? expand_more

Our technical specializations cover leading RPA tools like UiPath and Power Automate, along with Python for AI and machine learning. We focus on practical application and integration with enterprise systems.

What is the format of the courses? expand_more

We offer a flexible, blended learning model. Our courses include on-demand video lectures, hands-on labs, live Q&A sessions with instructors, and a community forum for peer support.

Is the certification recognized in the industry? expand_more

Yes, our certifications are highly valued by employers. They demonstrate a proven ability to design, build, and manage automation and AI solutions, giving your team members a competitive edge.
